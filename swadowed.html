Name

The name Swadowed comes from a silly and often childlike pronounciation of the serbian word for ice cream, sladoled. It reinforces the youthful image of the brand and the childlike joy in eating ice cream.

Colorway

Cold colors are meant to associate to a feeling of opening a cold freezer to reach for an ice cream pint, accentuated by saturated and bright colors.

Icon

The icon contains the beggining letters of the two words that together form the name of the brand, around which other elements construct an abstracted icon of an ice cream cone.

Pattern

The pattern is constructed out of the elements of the logo. The elements of the pattern are scattered in no organized manner as to reinforce the lively and youtful theme of the brand.
