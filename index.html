Car Dušan Pivo

A concept for light lager beer brand based on the likeness of the 14th century Serbian Tsar, Stephen Dušan. Inspired by Serbian medieval imagery and symbols of the Serbian Empire.

Dijamant

A rebrand concept for a Serbian edible oil and mayonnaise manufacturing company Dijamant. The company's name Dijamant, meaning diamond was a major source of inspiration.

Swadowed

A concept for an ice cream brand. Cold colors are meant to associate to a feeling of opening a cold freezer to reach for an ice cream pint, accentuated by saturated and bright colors. The name is a Serbian play on words.

OFK Lug

A rebrand for a small town lower league team in Veliki Mokri Lug, Serbia. The team founded in 2020 is proud to bring something new, fresh and different to the little leagues.

Sinđelić Niš

Founded in 1918, named after a Serbian revolution hero, Sinđelić is the oldest football club in Niš, the Heart of the Balkans. This design concept aims to refresh the club's imagery and bring Niš's oldest club to the 21st century.
