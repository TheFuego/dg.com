Car Dušan

The main icon of the brand is the reinterpreted face of the famous medieval Serbian Emperor Dušan. As there are no credible illustrations except for a few church frescoes, the face is reinterpreted liberally, in a sturdy way, with a lowered brow and almost a brash expression, reinforcing the Emperors nickname and most recognizable trait, might.

Colorway

The colors are derived from the flag of the medieval Serbian empire

Custom font

Inspired by medieval cyrillic manuscripts, this custom font brings a modern and sharp twist to the cyrillic scripts of old. The font is made in such a way to be highly adaptable to the latin alphabet if needed.

Bottle

The bottle is a European standard .475 long neck, featuring a long, narrow neck to allow for easy grip and shorter, flatter shoulders. This allows for a shorter transition between neck and body. The branding reflects on the bottle through and through, as it is tinted imperial maroon, with a classic-beer-label-inspired label sticker.

Bottlecap

Colored maroon, this bottlecap also contains a heraldic symbol of the medieval Serbian imperial crown. The crown, traditionally on a head of an emperor, symbolically crowns this beer.
